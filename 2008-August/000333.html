Hi,

Compiling any of the dependencies from source complicates things - 
requiring Makefile edits, etc.

All of these packages are available in the standard Ubuntu repositories, 
and, I assume also in the standard Fedora repositories.

Have a look at http://rpm.pbone.net/index.php3 for possible Fedora 
libvlc packages.

Regards,
Richard.
