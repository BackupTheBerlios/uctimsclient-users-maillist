Hi Prateek

Thanks for finding this bug. I forgot to add the sip: scheme to the 
p-cscf address. To fix it just change line 235 in 
ims_exosip_event_handler.c from

int s = eXosip_subscribe_build_initial_request(&subscribe, pref->impu, 
pref->impu, imsua_add_lr_to_route(pref->pcscf), "reg", 600000);

to this

int s = eXosip_subscribe_build_initial_request(&subscribe, pref->impu, 
pref->impu, imsua_add_lr_to_route(add_sip_scheme(pref->pcscf)), "reg", 
600000);

and it should work fine.

Regards

Dave
