To enable the parsing again, do not forget to remove following piece of 
code in watchers.c and presence.c:

notify_str[notify_len] = '\0';

Marco Happenhofer wrote:
> Hi,
> I have downloaded the latests Version of UCTIMSClient 1.0.11.
>

> 
> So I found a problem with the changing of the notifybody to a string.
> 
> I replaced all:
> osip_body_to_str(notify_body, &notify_str, &reg_len);

> notify_str = notify_body->body;
