Hi,
I have downloaded the latests Version of UCTIMSClient 1.0.11.

If I enable the presence service the client subscribes to all 
presentites and to the presence.winfo service. Interesting is that the 
Subscribe message for the presentites goes to the p-cscf and the 
Subscribe message for the presence.winfo service directly to the s-cscf.

I changed:
if(eXosip_subscribe_build_initial_request (&subscribe, pref->impu, 
pref->impu, imsua_add_lr_to_route(pref->pcscf), "presence.winfo", expires))
to:
if(eXosip_subscribe_build_initial_request (&subscribe, pref->impu, 
pref->impu, imsua_add_lr_to_route(add_sip_scheme(pref->pcscf)), 
"presence.winfo", expires))

I use:
Ubuntu 8.04
libosip2-2    3.0.3-2-1
libexosip2-4    3.0.3-3-1

Could somebody verify this problem?
