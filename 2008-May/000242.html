hi HAK

What dialog are you referring to here? Instant messaging conversation, 
voice call conversation, or other?

If you are referring to any session dialog, then some extra application 
server would be needed to store the necessary information and set all of 
this up again when Alice signs into terminal B but since the dialog is 
destroyed when one use leaves the session would it not break SIP rules 
by re-initiating the session? I am not that familiar with the RFC so 
perhaps you can check that.

In any case I don't believe the client does what you are looking for in 
this follow me service and we will happily try to accommodate any 
extensions to the client you may make in implementing such a service.
