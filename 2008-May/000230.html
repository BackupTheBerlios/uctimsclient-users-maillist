I have downloaded the latests Version of UCTIMSClient 1.0.11.

I had some problems with the Notify Messages. In the cases the client 
received the Notify successfully a segmentation fault occurred. And I 
remembered to older Version I used (1.0.9) that in the Registration 
State always some additional symbols were  displayed.

by
notify_str = notify_body->body;

Ubuntu 8.04
libosip2-2	3.0.3-2-1
libexosip2-4	3.0.3-3-1
