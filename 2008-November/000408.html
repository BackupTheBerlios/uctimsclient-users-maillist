Hi,

There is an incompatibility problem between the cdp branch of ser_ims 
and the latest trunk of ser_ims. 

Essentially a recent addition into the ser_ims trunk introduced a qop 
parameter into the registration procedure - the ser_ims cdp branch does 
not cater for this and incorrectly parses this qop parameter, creating a 
corrupted WWW-Authenticate header in the 401 Response to the Register 
message that results in a client crash.

The best way to fix this currently is to disable the qop parameter in 
the scscf.cfg file:
Replace:
modparam("scscf","registration_qop","auth,auth-int") with
modparam("scscf","registration_qop","")

Or if you are keen and have the time you can sift through the ser_ims 
cdp branch and bring it up to date with the ser_ims trunk regarding the 
qop parameter!!

Richard.
