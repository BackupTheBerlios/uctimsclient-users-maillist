There are a couple of example on the Internet for this, and it just depends
on how detailed you want to be. A silly example for Alice is given below:

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<resource-lists xmlns="urn:ietf:params:xml:ns:resource-lists">
    <list name="Friends">
    <display-name>My Friends</display-name>
        <entry uri="sip:bob at open-ims.test">
            <display-name>Bob</display-name>
        </entry>
        <entry uri="sip:mallory at open-ims.test">
            <display-name>Mallory</display-name>
        </entry>
    </list>
</resource-lists>

Take a look at the xml-schemas in the Openxcap installation for a better
understanding of how the documents are formatted.
