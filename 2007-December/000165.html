Hi Vincent

Please confirm that you have gstreamer 0.10 and the necessary plugins; 
try this line to test:

gst-launch-0.10 filesrc location=hostel.avi ! decodebin ! queue ! 
ffmpegcolorspace ! ximagesink

My guess from those error messages is that you are missing the ffmpeg 
plugins for gstreamer (gstreamer0.10-ffmpeg). Check that you have the 
ffmpegcolorspace plugin like this:

gst-inspect-0.10 | grep ffmpegcolorspace

Regards

Dave
