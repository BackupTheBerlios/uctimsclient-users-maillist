Hey, trying putting an HTTP address into your indirection server's
key-value pairs. This will then open a firefox window with the url that
you entered in the key/value tags. It's a quick way of knowing that your
client is at least receiving a proper URL. If that works, then it really
is an issue of the streaming server and your client not playing well
together.

Also, don't test your streams with the quicktime client, as this really
doesn't give you an indication of how VLC will play the streams.
Instead, make sure that you can play them in VLC, as this is what the
IMS client uses.

/bill
