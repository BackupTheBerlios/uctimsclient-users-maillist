Hi Li

This is an IMS feature. The user subscribes to their own registration 
state, so that if they are ever deregistered by the network they will be 
informed. This is more commonly known as the reg event. If you look at 
the reg event tab you will see the data from the NOTIFY message. This 
also serves to inform the user which other IMPUs are currently registered.

At present the client does not use this information for anything (except 
to display it), it's more just to test that the IMS core is working 
properly.

Regards

Dave
