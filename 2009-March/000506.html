Hi Jean-Philippe

The client uses the gStreamer libraries for audio and video. gStreamer
handles everything from capturing the audio from the microphone, encoding
the voice and transmitting the stream over RTP. If you want to find out how
the jitter buffer works you will need to dig around in the gStreamer source
code, not the client. You can see how the client invokes the gStreamer
library in gstreamer.c.

Regards

David
