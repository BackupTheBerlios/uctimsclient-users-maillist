Hi,

It is simply because Wireshark uses diameter defualt port when IMS core uses many different ports for each CSCFs.

You have to check your CSCFs xml files where the diameter peers are declared and add the corresponding diameter ports in the diameter protocol profile of wireshark.
i.e. the "Acceptor port".
