Hi

The behavior you are talking about is a fundamental part of SIP, i.e. 
retransmitting messages that do not receive a reply. It is the client 
that is sending these retransmissions.

Is there a reason why your AS is not sending any kind of reply to the 
client?

If you really want to stop this (and I really do not advise this) you 
must edit the source code of the eXosip SIP stack to stop the 
retransmissions. Performing this in the core network is not advisable 
either. Remember in SIP the intelligence lies in the clients not the 
network.

Regards

Dave
