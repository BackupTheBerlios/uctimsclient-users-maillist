Hi there,

I have analyzed some ACR, ACA, CCR and CCA messages captured by wireshark
from tests with UCT IMS client requesting a video in online and offline
mode.

I have identified the following discrepancies between my capture and the
3GPP 32.299 v10.1.0 (release 10) for the mandatory AVP :

I miss the following mandatory fields (M) in my messages :

Accounting Request  (ACR) :
- Destination-Realm
- Proxy-Host
- Proxy-State

Accounting Answer  (ACA) :
- Accounting-Record-Type
- Accounting-Record-Number
- Proxy-Host
- Proxy-State

Credit Control Request (CCR) :
- Destination-Realm
- Service-Context-Id
- CC-Request-Number
- Subscription-Id-Type
- Subscription-Id-Data
- Envelope-Start-Time
- QoS-Class-Identifier
- User-Equipment-Info-Type
- User-Equipment-Info-Value
- Proxy-Host Proxy-State

Credit Control Answer (CCA) :
- Auth-Application-Id
- CC-Request-Type
- CC-Request-Number
- G-S-U-Pool-Identifier
- CC-Unit-Type
- Unit-Value
- Value-Digits
- Final-Unit-Action
- Redirect-Address-Type
- Redirect-Server-Address
- Time-Quota-Type
- Base-Time-Interval
- Unit-Value
- Value-Digits
- Currency-Code
- Unit-Value
- Value-Digits
- Currency-Code
- Proxy-Host
- Proxy-State

I cannot figure out whether those parameter will really be necessary to my
charging or not.
Anybody can tell me if this is a show stopper ?
