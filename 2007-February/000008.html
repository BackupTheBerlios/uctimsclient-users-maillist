Hi Gaurav

That's a problem I had with alsa, having never used it before. 
Unfortunately I struggled with getting the right settings and eventually 
settled on some settings which worked for us. It's a good sign that you 
can hear the ringing.

I suggest changing the last two parameters in this line in media.c:

    initialise_alsa(pcm_handle, SND_PCM_FORMAT_MU_LAW, 8000, 4, 256);

until you find something that works. As you can see I already put in a 
bit of a hack to get an Ensoniq card working!

If anyone has any experience with alsa please let us know how to setup 
the card properly.

Regards

Dave
