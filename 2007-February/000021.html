I know there are issues with some sound cards not working perfectly, but 
you have a strange problem. The RTP packets should flow anyway.

Make sure you are capturing on port 10001 (the default client media 
port). Typing tcpdump should verify that there are a large number of 
packets flowing of length 172.

It's possible the client could not open your sound card at all and gave up.

Dave
