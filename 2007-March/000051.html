Hi Naga

If your DNS is setup correctly as in the openIMScore installation 
instructions, you should set your P-CSCF to sip:pcscf.open-ims.test:4060

You can test your DNS by trying to ping pcscf.open-ims.test and see if 
you get a response.

If not the core probably won't work anyway, but you can try: 
sip:127.0.0.1:4060

- Dave
